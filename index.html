About RSSA Computer Center & Library

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2005, RSSA Computer Center & Library has been at the forefront of digital education and literary resources in our community. We pride ourselves on creating an inclusive space where technology and knowledge meet to empower individuals of all ages.

Our vision is to become the leading educational hub where students, professionals, and community members can access cutting-edge technology and a vast collection of resources to fuel their personal and professional growth.

A Treasure Trove of Knowledge

Our library houses over 4,000 titles across various disciplines including computer science, business, literature, and general knowledge. We continuously update our collection to serve our community's evolving needs.

Enjoy our quiet reading zones, group study rooms, and digital resource stations designed to enhance your learning experience.

Library Services:

  • Access to digital resources including e-books and academic journals
  • Research assistance from trained staff
